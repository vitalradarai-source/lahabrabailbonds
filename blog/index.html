Bail Bonds La Habra: What Happens After an Arrest at La Habra Police Department Jail

When someone is arrested in La Habra, they are booked at the La Habra Police Department Jail at 150 N Euclid St — a short-term facility that holds most detainees up to 72 hours before transfer to Orange County Jail.
